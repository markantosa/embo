======================================================================
1. SYSTEM OVERVIEW
======================================================================
EMBO is a closed-loop embolic agent preparation device. The MCU board integrates three sensor subsystems (UAS, CV camera, StallGuard viscosity), two stepper motor actuators, two mechanical limit switches, and a UI breakout board connected via IDC ribbon cable.

1.1 Why ESP32-S3-WROOM-1-N8
---------------------------
Module: chip, 8MB flash, 40MHz crystal, PCB antenna ‚Äî no external MCU support components needed
Built-in USB Serial/JTAG on GPIO19/20 ‚Äî no bridge IC needed
Bluetooth LE 5.0 on-chip ‚Äî NimBLE for wireless debug
36 GPIO broken out ‚Äî 28 used in v2.4, 8 free
N8 variant: no PSRAM ‚Äî GPIO35‚Äì37 free for SPI bus

8. UI INTERFACE SYSTEM ‚Äî BREAKOUT BOARD VIA IDC RIBBON
======================================================================
All UI components (ILI9341 TFT, EC11 rotary encoder, 2 push buttons, passive piezo buzzer) are on a separate breakout PCB. The main board connects to the breakout via a 20-conductor 2.54mm IDC ribbon cable. This keeps high-frequency SPI switching noise away from the analog UAS section on the main board.

10. PCB LAYOUT RULES
======================================================================

10.1 Layer stack
----------------
2-layer FR4 1oz copper ENIG. Top: all SMD + signal routing. Bottom: ground plane + power routing.
UAS analog signals on top layer only ‚Äî no vias in UAS paths.

10.2 Antenna keepout
--------------------
No copper within 15mm of ESP32 antenna on ALL layers. Remove bottom copper in keepout zone.
Place module at board edge with antenna outward.

10.3 Power section
------------------
Q1 AO4407A: D7 Zener cathode to Source, anode to Gate, within 3mm of Q1.
SS34A catch diode within 5mm of LM2596 SW pin. Snubber 10Œ©+1nF within 2mm of SW pin.
AMS1117: SOT-223 tab to ‚â•100mm¬≤ copper pour + ‚â•4 thermal vias.

10.4 UAS chain
--------------
IC1 (Tx) and IC2 (Rx): ‚â•15mm separation. Keep motor traces away.
J2 ‚Üí R_in ‚Üí D1 ‚Üí IC2: under 20mm, no vias, GND guard each side.
C_adc within 2mm of GPIO1 pad. C_env within 5mm of IC2 ch-B output.

10.5 TMC2209
------------
QFN-28: hot plate reflow with paste + stencil. VCP 100nF within 2mm per IC. Thermal pad GND continuity verified after reflow.
VM decoupling (100¬µF + 100nF) directly at VM pin per IC ‚Äî not shared.
R_PDN_UP (10kŒ©): place near UART junction node.

10.6 IDC ribbon connector (J9)
------------------------------
Place J9 at board edge ‚Äî ribbon needs to exit the enclosure to reach breakout board.
100nF cap from TOUCH_IRQ trace (IDC pin 12) to GND on main board ‚Äî within 5mm of J9.
Route SPI signals (IDC pins 5/6/7) away from TMC2209 motor outputs and UAS analog area.
3.3V and 5V ribbon traces: ‚â•0.3mm width to handle breakout board current load.

10.7 USB-C routing
------------------
D+ (GPIO20): connect to both A6 and B6 pads of USB-C connector as single net.
D‚àí (GPIO19): connect to both A7 and B7 pads of USB-C connector as single net.
CC1 and CC2 resistors within 5mm of connector.
